About Justin Bieber

Justin Bieber was born Canadian on March 1st, 1994. He became well known with his song baby which for the longest time was the most disliked video of all times on YouTube.
Nowadays, Justin is happily married to Hailey Rhodes Bieber and is working on new music, whilst trying to maintain healthy.

The sound of Justin Bieber

Justin's music can mostly be described as pop with some sidepaths along the way. As for example, his album Journals has more of an R&B-sound to it. As of his latest single, you can here a bit of gospel in it.
